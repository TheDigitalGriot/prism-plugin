Ensemble mode

superposition → collapse
Your intended workflow. Drop one task; N models run it in parallel — each in its own worktree, blind to the others. They diverge (superposition). Then Valence scores the candidates and you collapse to the best — pick one, or merge the strongest parts. The seam here is the collapse, not a handoff.
