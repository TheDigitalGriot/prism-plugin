THE MARK: A bold wireframe polyhedron prism interpreted through subtle impossible-geometry logic, nested inside a sharp diamond / shield outline. The outer diamond/shield border is bold white with sharp clean edges.

The geometry should imply depth and tension through overlapping planes and negative space — but remain completely flat vector.
